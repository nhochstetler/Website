Product Strategy: Air Defense

The Lightweight Air Defense Interceptor blurs the line between missile and aircraft. It is an ultra-low-cost, runway-independent aircraft designed solely to carry and launch two AIM-9X Sidewinders. Capable of vertical takeoff and landing (VTOL), it autonomously detects, engages, and neutralizes airborne threats before returning to base for rapid rearming and redeployment.

Targeting Optimization Software

Coded and built myself with Python, this targeting software is an optimization algorithm that ingests real-time data from numerous friendly assets and enemy targets to autonomously assign engagements. It maximizes combat effectiveness by determining the most efficient pairing of shooters to targets, minimizing response time and resource usage while maximizing mission success. The system is adaptable for both maritime and air operations, making it ideal for coordinated fleet defense, aerial intercepts, and joint-domain engagements.
